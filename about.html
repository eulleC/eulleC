About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a recent graduate at Augustana College. I graduated with a major in Geology and Geography and a minor in GIS and Technology. While in school I participated in all sorts of projects, ranging from stream microplastics all the way to national level volcano threat assessments. I was born in the Philippines but am now based in Illinois. Outside of school, you can find me playing video games and watching soccer. I also enjoy collecting vintage maps! Among my collection, old USGS topographic maps and National Geographic maps are my favorites!
