About Me

I am a student at Augustana College pursuing majors in Geology and Geography with a minor in GIS and Technology. My passion lies in creating meaningful visualizations and maps that tell compelling stories about our world.

With a background in both natural sciences and digital technology, I bring a unique perspective to cartographic design and geographical analysis.

Research Projects

IMPROVING THE EXPOSURE CALCULATION FOR THE VPI30 COMPONENT OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL VOLCANIC THREAT ASSESSMENT USING SENTINEL-2 IMAGERY DATA AND GIS EXTRACTION METHODS

  • Completed analysis to place large population dataset to its respective study areas
  • Extracted built area pixels from Sentinel-2 imagery data
  • Compared current exposure calculation vs my improved exposure calculation to identify differences in quality
  • Results will be verified by the Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology to be part of the Philippine National Volcanic Threat Assessment report
  • Will be presented at the Geological Society of America 2025 Joint North East – North Central Section Meeting, Eerie, PA

UBIQUITOUS MICROPLASTICS IN FLUVIAL SEDIMENTS OF URBAN STREAM SYSTEMS IN DAVENPORT AND BETTENDORF, IOWA (2024)

  • Completed field work in multiple study sites to collect sediment samples
  • Completed lab work to isolate microplastics from sediment samples
  • Used fluorescent microscopy to identify microplastics in sediments
  • Presented at the Geological Society of America 2024 Joint North East - South Central Section Meeting, Springfield, MO

STREAM ASSESSMENT OF MICROPLASTICS IN QUAD CITY WATERSHEDS (2023)
