About Me

I am a student at Augustana College pursuing majors in Geology and Geography with a minor in GIS and Technology. My passion lies in creating meaningful visualizations and maps that tell compelling stories about our world.

With a background in both natural sciences and digital technology, I bring a unique perspective to cartographic design and geographical analysis.

Research Projects

Digital Cartography and Design

I specialize in crafting detailed and informative maps that combine scientific data with aesthetic appeal. My work includes a comprehensive bathymetric map of Indonesia, a detailed political map of Southeast Asia, and a Metro Manila transportation analysis visualization.

GIS Analysis
